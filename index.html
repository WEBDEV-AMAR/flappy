About Flappy Flame

Flappy Flame is an exciting skill game where you control a magical flame through challenging obstacles. Test your reflexes and timing as you navigate through increasingly difficult gaps. Compete with friends and try to beat your high score in this addictive HTML5 game!

How to play: Click, tap, or press Space to make the flame jump. Avoid hitting the pipes and try to survive as long as possible!
